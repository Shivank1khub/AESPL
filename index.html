OUR SERVICES

Defence Careers Academy is a subsidiary of Arun Educational Services Private Limited (AESPL), which is registered under company act 1956. The institute provides coaching for aspiring candidate to join Indian Defence Services, and other forces like BSF, CRPF, SSB, ITBP, CISF, Assam rifles RPF, UP Police and Delhi Police, and Railways. This academy also provides free of coast coaching to the wards of martyrs of defence forces & other forces, who died during the operations on duty. There is a discount of 40% for the wards of serving soldiers and retired ones. More over in order to uplift the less fortunate section of the society, 35% discount is offered for the BPL card holders/children.

FOUNDER and PATRON
Brigadier Raj Kumar Chauhan,VSM
Brigadier Raj Kumar Chauhan is an Ex Jat Regiment officer of Indian Army. He has successfully commanded “4 Jat Regiment”, one Infantry Brigade and Sub Area.
