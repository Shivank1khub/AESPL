Mission
Mission of the Academy is to provide success based quality coaching for candidates who are aspiring to join Armed Forces. We see and understand that the top notches have the best of options and more facilities. Our focus is to develop and enhance the candidates from humble background.Our efforts are driven to ensure that our candidates successfully make it to become a part of Indian Forces.

THE EDUCATIONAL SERVICES INCLUDE
  • Free Career counseling for all the service levels & free Medical Examination.
  • Preparatory assistance to qualify written Examinations of NDA, Naval Academy and CDS.
  • Preparatory assistance to the female candidates for National Defence Academy (NDA), Naval Academy, CDS, TGS, UES, & NCC Entry.
  • 4 Preparatory assistance to qualify Pilot Aptitude Test.
  • Preparatory assistance to qualify written examination and interviews for Assistant Commandants of all Central forces.
  • Preparatory assistance to qualify Physical Training Medical Examination, Written examination and Interviews for JCO Religious Teacher, Havaildar- Education, JCO Catering and Surveyor Auto catographer of the Indian Army.
  • Preparatory assistance to qualify Physical Training Medical Examination and Written examination of GD, Head constable (Ministerial, Radio Operator) Assistant Sub Inspector constable (General Duty, Ministerial, Radio Operator) of BSF, CRPF, ITBP, SSB, CISF, Assam Rifles, RPF, Coastguard, Delhi Police and Uttar Pradesh Police.
  • Preparatory assistance to qualify Physical Training, Medical Examination and Written examination of Soldier General Duty, Soldier Clerk/Storekeeper Technical, Soldier Technical, Soldier Nursing Assistant, Soldier Non Matric and Soldier Tradesman of Indian Army.
  • Preparatory assistance to qualify Physical Training, Medical Examination and Written examination Airman Group X And Y of Indian Air force.
  • Preparatory assistance to qualify Physical Training Medical Examination and Written examination of Artificer Apprentice(AA), Direct Entry Diploma Holder(DEDH), Senior Secondary Recruits (SSR) Matric Entry Recruit(MER), Musician and Non Matric Recruit.
  • Preparatory assistance to qualify Entrance examination of Military School, Sainik School, Rashtriya Indian Military College and Navodya Vidyalaya.
  • Preparatory assistance to qualify Written examination and interviews for all posts of Banks and Railways.
